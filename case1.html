About

Reawote is an online platform that features a vast collection of PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials, 3D models, and HDRI images. We collaborated with Reawote to redesign their platform with a focus on improving both the user experience and visual interface. The previous design faced several key challenges: it lacked some of the new features users were actively requesting, it was not accessible to all users, and its outdated visual style did not reflect the high quality of the digital products being offered. Additionally, the design was inconsistent and unscalable, making future development difficult. To address these issues, I created a modern, cohesive design system that ensured scalability and consistency across the platform. I also introduced accessibility improvements and designed new features, including a streamlined process for users to request digitalizations, ultimately enhancing the platform’s functionality and aligning it with user needs and current design standards.
