About

Reawote is an online platform that features a vast collection of PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials, 3D models, and HDRI images. We collaborated with Reawote to create their design system, focusing on improving the platform's user experience and interface. Additionally, I designed new processes and features, such as enabling users to request digitalizations.
